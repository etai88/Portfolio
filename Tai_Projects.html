In the race car project, my team and I were were asked to create a race car using only recycled material. The car can have a maximum of twelve inches of string and twelve inches of tape. Also, it must be powered by a mouse trap. Another requirement is that it must go at least 4 meters. Our car went 8 meters which was the furthest in the class. We had to model the car and the mousetrap used to power the car in inventor. I assigned task for each person in my team as well as doing task myself. This project helped me learn how to be a better leader and team member and also improved my skills in Autodesk Inventor.

In the solar hydrogen vehicle project, my team and I were were asked to create a car that is powered by a hydrogen fuel cell and by a solar cell. We would then run test on the cars using either the hydrogen fuel cell or the solar cell and make a conclusion on which power source is more efficient. A few constraints were that the car itself must be at most 5”x12” and must be made using VEX parts and banana plugs. In this projects, I helped with building and breadboarding as well as running test. I also worked on documentation. All together, the team worked well together and produced a very well made car. Our car received positive feedback and compliments.
